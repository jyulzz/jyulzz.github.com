Context

Time zones are tricky business.

When you work remotely in teams spread accross the globe, it is important to know what time it is for your other team mates, whether to schedule meetings or for a quick chat.

Between daylight savings times, the date-change line, changes in the zones' boundaries and the rest, it can get confusing.

While collaborating within remote teams using Slack, I use TeamTimeZone Pro to keep track of what time it is for other team members.

It works by generating an image preview of the current timezones members of a channel or group are in, from which you can access a web application with more functionalities.

While this works great for planning events, is not as ideal for an at-a-glance look to make sure I am not waiting for a colleague for who it is 4:00 in the morning

I was missing a quick, integrated way to get a look at what time it is for my team mates.

So I decided to design one!
