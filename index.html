Profile

I am a people-lover, a communicator, an unabashed optimist and a curious experimenter.

As a designer, I believe that we deserve beautiful and efficient solutions in every aspect of our lives.

Through challenges, I refine core skills in the fields of design and user experience while also acquiring peripheral capacities that allow me to work closely with other specialists in IT, marketing, communication, social media and more.

Having run a startup has taught me self-reliance, the importance of effective collaboration and the power of trust.

On my own time, I love to travel and get lost in translation, cook for friends and burn the calories off with a long cycling session.

Stack
