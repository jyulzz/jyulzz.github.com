*From unforgettable memories, I drew these pictures.

This set of 6 illustrations reflect memories of time I spent in Seoul, South Korea.
They are rendered in a brutalist style, in reference to the many examples of this aesthetic found throughout the city. Seoul's neighborhoods all have their distinct atmosphere, but city-wide, the landscape is a mix of fiery mercantilism, efficient brutalism and a brand of cuteness unmistakably darker and meatier than Japan's. Let's not forget the night-time version, a much more livable version of Blade Runner's, where only the taxis crossing red lights are out to get you.
