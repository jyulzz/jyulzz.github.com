잊을 수 없는 추억으로 이 그림들을 그렸습니다…

This set of 6 illustrations reflect memories of time I spent in Seoul, South Korea.
They are rendered in a brutalist style, in reference to the many examples of this style found throughout the city. Seoul's neighborhoods all have their distinct atmosphere, but city-wide, the landscape is a mix of fiery mercantilism, efficient brutalism and a brand of cuteness slightly darker and meatier than Japan's. Let's not forget the night-time version, a much more livable version of Blade Runner's.
